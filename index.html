Franchise Process

Follow these 4 simple steps to become an Authorized Study Center (ASC) of Lifeway Computers.

1

Fill Application Form

Visit our "Downloads" section, get the Franchise Form, fill it with your center details, and email it to us or submit via the Contact page.

2

Document Verification

Submit KYC documents (Aadhar, PAN), Center Photos (Lab, Classroom, Reception), and Address Proof. Our team will verify them within 24 hours.

3

Fee Payment

Pay the Franchise Registration Fee online. We offer low-cost affiliation models for rural areas to promote digital literacy.

4

Authorization & Login

Receive your unique Center Code, Authorization Certificate, and Login Credentials to access the Center Admin Panel.

About Lifeway Computers

Lifeway Computer Education is a premier ISO 9001:2015 certified institute registered under the Govt. of India. We are dedicated to providing high-quality computer education to students across urban and rural India at affordable costs.

Established with a vision to eradicate computer illiteracy, we have grown into a vast network of authorized study centers. Our curriculum is designed by industry experts to ensure our students are job-ready from day one.

We offer a wide range of courses including ADCA, DCA, Web Design, Accounting, and Hardware Networking. Our certification is recognized nationwide, opening doors to employment in both government and private sectors.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide professional technical education to the grassroots level of society. We aim to empower youth by providing them with the skills needed to thrive in the digital economy.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted education network, bridging the digital divide and creating a knowledgeable, skilled workforce for the future of our nation.

Our Dream

"We dream of an India where every child, regardless of their economic background, has access to a computer. We envision a society where digital literacy is not a luxury, but a fundamental right. Our dream is to see our students leading the IT revolution globally."
